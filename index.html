About the Summit

The ASEAN Summit 2025 brings together leaders and stakeholders to accelerate sustainable development, address climate challenges, and foster inclusive economic growth across the region.

Objectives

  • Strengthen regional cooperation on climate resilience.
  • Mobilize public and private financing for green projects.
  • Promote inclusive economic recovery post-pandemic.

Host City

Kuala Lumpur — Malaysia's capital and a hub for diplomacy and culture.

Organizers

ASEAN Secretariat in partnership with the Government of Malaysia.

Accessibility & Inclusion

Venue is wheelchair accessible and live captioning will be available for major plenary sessions.
